Perfect for spicy sandwiches that will please anyone who loves Buffalo chicken wings

Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Sandwiches are a delicious and easy-to-make meal. To make them, simply place chicken breasts, buffalo sauce, and seasonings in a slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours. Once the chicken is tender and cooked through, shred it with two forks and mix in some additional buffalo sauce. Serve the shredded chicken on buns with your choice of toppings, such as lettuce, tomato, and blue cheese dressing. These sandwiches are perfect for game day or anytime you're craving a spicy and savory meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 (17.5 fluid ounce) bottle Buffalo wing sauce, divided
  • 0.5 (1 ounce) package dry ranch salad dressing mix
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 6 hoagie rolls, split lengthwise

steps

Step1
Place chicken breasts into the slow cooker; pour in 3/4 of the wing sauce and ranch dressing mix
step2
Cover and cook on Low for 6 to 7 hours.
step3
Shred chicken in the cooker with two forks. Stir in butter.
step4
Pile shredded chicken and sauce onto hoagie rolls. Serve with remaining Buffalo sauce.
