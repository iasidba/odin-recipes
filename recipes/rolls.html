Classic Dinner Rolls

Classic Dinner Rolls are a type of bread commonly served with dinner. They are known for their soft and fluffy texture, making them a popular choice for sandwiches or as a side dish. The rolls are typically made from a mixture of flour, yeast, sugar, salt, butter, and milk. They are often shaped into small balls or crescents before being baked until golden brown. Classic Dinner Rolls can be enjoyed warm or at room temperature, and are a delicious addition to any meal.
